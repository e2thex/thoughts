## What do we know about $R_0$

If we look at the key properties of $R_p$s, we can see that $R_0$ does not have these properties so: 
1. $R_0$ is not abstract and
2. $R_0$ is not probabilistic

## $R_0$'s free will

Was we discussed in [[On Free Will]], the construct of free will is dependent on having probabilistic possible paths; as $R_0$ is not probabilistic; it would not experience free will.
